## Working Papers

2. "Structural Transformation and Financial Deregulation" (with [Gang Zhang](https://english.ckgsb.edu.cn/faculty/zhang-gang/) and [Shengxing Zhang](https://sites.google.com/site/oo7zsx/home)), 2024. *Draft coming soon*
